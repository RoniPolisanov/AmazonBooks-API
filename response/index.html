GET BOOKS DETAILS BY LANGUAGE AND AUTHOR

Request Method: GET

Response: All the English/Spanish/Russian language books in the must-read library

URL: https://amazon-book.heroku.com/getBookDetailsByAuthor/language/author

Required Parameters: (1)language, (2)author

Required Values: language - "English", "Spanish", "Russian" | author - "George Orwell", "Stephen Hawking", "Dave Eggers", "Antonovsky Miriola", "Tom Kinjs", "Eduard Ben Israel", "Arkadi Putin" etc.

Example: http://amazon-book.heroku.com/getBookDetailsByAuthor/Russian/Vladimir%20Petrovsky

WARNING: Where a space sign appears you must place a "%20"
